The Unicorn

About

Features: Horse-like creature with a single horn on its forehead.
Abilities: Often associated with magic, healing, and purity.
Wings? ❌ No wings.
Horn? ✅ Yes.
Origin: Mythology, folklore, and fantasy stories.
Example: Commonly seen in Western mythology and in shows like My Little Pony.

The Alicorn

About

Features: A combination of unicorn and pegasus — a horse with both a horn and wings.
Abilities: Typically has both magic and flight abilities.
Wings? ✅ Yes.
Horn? ✅ Yes.
Origin: The term "alicorn" originally referred to the horn of a unicorn, but in modern fantasy (especially My Little Pony), it now refers to a winged unicorn.
Example: Princess Celestia or Twilight Sparkle (after transformation) in My Little Pony.

The Pegasus

About

Features: Horse with wings.
Abilities: Flight, sometimes enhanced speed or strength.
Wings? ✅ Yes.
Horn? ❌ No.
Origin: Greek mythology — Pegasus is the name of a specific winged horse.
Example: Often shown as a noble steed or celestial being.
